The Framework

The Psychology of
High-Leverage Leadership.

Most conventional leadership coaching focuses heavily on external skills: how to run a meeting, how to delegate, or how to manage a P&L. At DoneCoaching, we understand that external skills are entirely useless if the internal operating system is flawed. We focus entirely on state control, psychological resilience, and strategic architecture.

Our proprietary framework is built squarely on the intersection of advanced behavioral psychology, neuro-linguistic programming (NLP), and high-performance business scaling. We do not just look at your calendar; we look at the cognitive biases and mental bottlenecks that are subconsciously stalling your momentum and capping your income.

By fundamentally shifting how your brain processes risk, reward, and conflict under high pressure, we create a clear, actionable path toward radical efficiency. You will transition from being a time-poor manager constantly putting out daily fires, into a highly effective visionary who directs energy, influences stakeholders, and commands the room.

Step-By-Step Journey

A Legacy-Building
Transformation Roadmap.

We do not believe in open-ended, aimless consulting. Our Executive Coaching program is a highly structured, aggressive 6-month sprint. Every single month is heavily regimented to ensure we identify, attack, and definitively resolve the core issues holding back your organization's ultimate growth.

Month 1

The Brutal Audit

We begin with a deep, objective diagnostic. This isn't just a basic personality survey; it's a 360° behavioral audit involving key stakeholders, direct reports, and your own internal reflections. We identify the "Invisible Ceilings" currently capping your revenue and establish the hard KPI metrics for our engagement. We map exactly where your time is bleeding out and plug the holes immediately.

Month 2-3

Architectural Design

With the bottlenecks identified, we rebuild your operational schedule and leadership style from the ground up. We implement advanced energy-management protocols and high-ticket communication frameworks. This phase is heavily focused on dismantling old, comfortable habits and forcefully installing a new, highly scalable operating system for your career and personal life.

Month 4-5

Peak Execution

This is where the heavy lifting occurs. We take the new frameworks and apply them directly to your highest-stakes environments. We focus on advanced negotiation, board alignment, and public stage presence. Whether you are leading a hostile boardroom meeting or delivering a massive industry keynote, we refine your authority and influence through live feedback and intense simulation loops.

Month 6

Legacy Integration

We finalize the handoff. Coaching should not be a permanent crutch. We ensure your new frameworks are permanent, self-sustaining, and successfully adopted by your downstream management. You exit the program with a personalized 3-year growth strategy, clear defensive mechanisms to prevent regression, and permanent access to the Executive Vault.
